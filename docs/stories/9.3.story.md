## Status

## Story

**As a** visitor,  
**I want** a pricing page with plans and feature comparison,  
**so that** I can choose the plan that fits my needs and subscribe.

1. **AC1**: Page `/pricing` avec plans (Free, Pro, Elite).
2. **AC2**: Pricing tiers : mensuel/trimestriel/annuel.
3. **AC3**: Feature comparison : tableau comparatif détaillé (Free vs Pro vs Elite).
4. **AC4**: CTA : "Start Free Trial" / "Subscribe" (liens vers signup/checkout).
5. **AC5**: Performance : page load < 2s, SEO score > 90 (Lighthouse).
6. **AC6**: Responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop).
